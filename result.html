SafeBridge compares your asset to the S&P500 using live volatility (CoinGecko) and layers in optional SoSoValue signals when you pick an SSI (SoSoValue Index).

  • Risk score — driven mainly by recent volatility vs a ~13.5% S&P500 baseline, plus your quiz bias if you took it.
  • SBSI — a simplified “index-investor comfort” score; small nudges from ETF flows (MAG7 path) and short-term index momentum when available.
  • SoSoValue card — official index id/price/24h move from SoSoValue; news counts filtered by the basket’s top weight; Attention is our lightweight engagement read from those headlines.
  • Institutional Activity — US-listed ETF aggregate for the anchor symbol (e.g. BTC); not cash flows for the SSI basket itself—tap “What’s this?” there.

SSI intelligence —
Index price (SoSoValue)
—
SoSoValue’s index market snapshot price for the resolved API id (e.g. ssiMAG7). It is their quoted index level in USD—separate from the CoinGecko proxy we use for volatility math above.
24h momentum
—
Pulled from the same snapshot (fields such as 24h_change_pct). We show percent move plus a tiny label (Risk-on / Neutral / Risk-off)—these are SafeBridge heuristics for skimming, not trading advice.
News matches (feed total)
—
Count from SoSoValue’s /news feed when filtered by the largest basket constituent’s currency_id. The “filter BITCOIN” tag tells you which leg drove that query—it’s broader narrative volume, not MAG7-specific subscriptions.
Attention index
—
A SafeBridge 0–100 proxy computed from likes vs impressions on the returned news rows. It captures headline engagement, not a SoSoValue official sentiment score.

Constituents, snapshot, and news are fetched from SoSoValue Open API (indices + news endpoints).
